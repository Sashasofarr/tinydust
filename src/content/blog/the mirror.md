---
title: "The mirror"
description: "The new beginning"
pubDate: 'July 17 2026'
---
<div class="poem">it's in this mirror
i see you
i see me
the guilt in you that won't let go
the accountability you're not ready for
the shame i try to bury
the pain i say doesn't exist
but the mirror never lies
however distant it might be
you feel it pulling 
in stillness, in dreams
right here when we try to wish it away
but the mirror never lies
beyond the despair
hides the pale green crane
the one i folded
the one you lost
it might turn up one day, still
as long as 
you look into the mirror
you see me
you see you
</div>
